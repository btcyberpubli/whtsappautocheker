📊 ENDPOINTS IMPLEMENTADOS:

   POST   /api/whatsapp-alert          Recibir alerta
          Payload: {type, contactName, chatId, lastMessage, detectedAt, ...}
          Response: {success, alertId, isDuplicate, ...}

   GET    /api/whatsapp-alerts         Obtener todas las alertas
          Query: ?status=active|resolved|ignored (opcional)
          Response: {success, count, activeCount, alerts, ...}

   PUT    /api/whatsapp-alert/:id      Actualizar estado
          Payload: {status: "resolved|ignored|active"}
          Response: {success, message, alertId, ...}
